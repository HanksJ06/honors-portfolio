About ME

My name is Joseph Hankins.

I am pursing an Bachler's in Information technology specliising in Networking, and Game Design.
From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by the constant evolution of technology. In my free time, I enjoy diving into new and interesting developments in Information Technology. I’m especially passionate about figuring out how tech can solve real problems and make meaningful improvements in people’s lives. Whenever I learn about the latest advancements, I can’t help but imagine creative ways they could be used to build a better future. At the same time, I have a deep appreciation for nature. I love taking walks to soak in the beauty of the outdoors and reflect on the balance that exists in the world around us. These two passions—technology and nature—have shaped who I am. While I value both, I believe technology is the path that can help me create the future I dream of. Throughout school, I’ve always given my best effort to set myself up for success, and I’m determined to keep that momentum going. My main goal is to secure a career that aligns with my interests and lets me keep exploring these passions. Ultimately, I want to make a real difference in the world and leave it better than I found it.
